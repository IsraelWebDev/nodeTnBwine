The future is here. Try & Buy Wine is the next generation wine shop

a modern, minimalist space, centered around high-tech glass-enclosed wine dispensing machines that provide samples of ALL the wines on offer.
The ground floor "Tasting Room" is a high-tech wine showroom for the products and includes tablet computers enabling customers to learn about the offerings.
The lower "Cellar" level includes a glass enclosed climate-controlled environment for inventory storage as well as wine lockers available to members for rent.
The "Cellar" level also offers wine-tasting classes, private parties, sales of personal wine-dispensing machines and private wine cellar consulting.
